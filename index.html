In the Spring

This time of year we are checking for swarms and, occasionally, catching swarms. John Aschenbrenner is on the Oregon State Beekeepers swarm call list. If you are in the St. Johns or Linnton areas and see a swarm gathered within reach, please call us at 503-283-4042. Check out our Facebook page for additional updates.
