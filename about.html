About Asches Urban Honey LLC

John and Mary Ann Aschenbrenner

Just a few blocks from the St. Johns' town center, Asches Urban Honey LLC is a family business that specializes in sweet local honey from our own organically tended bees. Proprieters, John and Mary Ann Aschenbrenner, help ensure honey bee health by leaving plenty of the hard-earned nectar on the hives after the fall harvest and allowing the hives to re-queen naturally.

Asches Urban Honey LLC is bottled straight from the comb without filters, heating or pasturization of any kind.

When John is not tending bees or helping patients with apitherapy, he is employeed at NW Container Corp., also located in St. Johns. Mary Ann is President of Waterlink Web, an interactive agency focused on the needs of small businesses that are ready to grow.
