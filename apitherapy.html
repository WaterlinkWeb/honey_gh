Apitherapy, the medicinal use of products made by honeybees

Products of the honeybee include bee venom, honey, pollen, royal jelly, propolis and beeswax. Asches Urban Honey LLC specializes in bee venom therapy, as practiced in ancient Egypt, Greece and China, to treat arthritis and other joint problems.

The sting of our organically tended honeybees will engage the power of your body to heal itself. The sting itself works as a type of needleless acupuncture. The venom draws nutrient rich blood to the afflicted joint, cleaning and revitalizing. Various treatments are available including micro-stings, mini-stings and full stings.

Initial consultation includes testing for allergic reaction and discussion of recommended therapy. John Aschenbrenner, is a registered member of The American Apitherapy Society.
